Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I apply for admission?

Applications are submitted online through our portal. Visit the Admissions page for requirements, deadlines, and the step-by-step process.

When is the application deadline?

Deadlines vary by program. For Fall 2024, most programs have a January 15 deadline. Check specific program pages for exact dates.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

Campus tours are available Monday-Friday. Schedule online through our Visit page or contact the Admissions Office directly.

Who do I contact for IT issues?

For technical support, email itsupport@university.edu or call our help desk at +237 6XX XXX XXX during business hours.
